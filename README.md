# MoreTradition - Reborn

Once [MoreTradition](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2616955720) is a rare and excellent mod, not only in the variety of ways to play, but the tradition adds more than just BUFFs, but a more immersive gameplay. This is what players value the most.

But after the release of version 3.4, [星劫](https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561198277755366) was too busy studying for his exams to continue updating the MOD, I tried to get in touch with 星劫 in the comments section and in private messages on steam to join the MOD maintenance and I tried to get in touch with 星劫 in the comments section and in a private steam message to join the mod maintenance and update work, but unfortunately, I couldn't get a reply from the original author.

Therefore, I will temporarily release a MoreTradition - Reborn mod adapted to the latest version of Stellaris so that players of the new version can still play the MoreTradition mod, and when 星劫 has time to continue to complete his updates, I will remove this mod.

## Adjustments relative to the new version

**Artifact Action**

* [Adjusted] Duration of Heritage Tradition: Appreciation for All, Heritage Tradition: High Profile Exhibition, and Heritage Tradition: Herald Experience is modified to hourly years, and will immediately gain a certain amount of cohesion and influence (as the original Heritage Action decision).

**Ship Weapons**

* [3.6] Brilliant Energy Emitter deals double damage to Battleships and triple damage to Titans, just like other XL slot weapons.

** Planet Decision**

* [Adjusted] One Mind Tradition: One Mind is no longer limited in duration, but takes effect when enabled until canceled. Removing One Heart Tradition will cost 100 influences and will lose 1 influence for each population on the planet.

**Decree**

* [3.4] Decrees now consume Cohesion instead of Influence.
* [Adjusted] Decree: Special Training can choose to train 1/5/10/25 leaders at a time, and the Promising Genius trait has been supplemented with all obtainable traits (excluding character-specific traits such as Great Khan, Nano Individual, and Oracle).
* [3.4] Decree Tradition: Vertical and Decree Tradition: Majestic has been modified to last indefinitely, but requires Cohesion to maintain.

**Post**

* [FIXED] Fixed a priority bug with elite posts offered by Capital Traditions, where the population would induct these posts on their own and with a higher priority than lower-level posts of the same type.

**Traditions**

* [Adjusted] All traditions using negative effects were replaced with positive effects, spending cohesion to get DEBUFF hard to understand.
* [3.4] rhapsody: modified the function of each perk to make it adapt to the new version of the giant structure cost (cohesion).
* [Tweak] Army: increased collateral damage for squad collaboration perk changed to reduced collateral damage.
* [Tweak] Psychic: Void output perk has been changed to increase energy coin output by 20% as a percentage instead of a fixed 20 points.
* [3.4] Decree: Training System perk changed to reduce empire size penalty, Smart Administration perk changed to reduce empire size from population and zoning, Flexible Institutions perk changed to reduce empire size from star systems and colonies, Streamlined System perk changed to reduce decree cost and maintenance fee, Widely Recognized perk changed to increase decree funding and reduce decree cost and maintenance fee.

**Population Traits**

* [FIXED] Artificial Psychic/Special Proprietor traits will not show up directly in the trait list, but can only be added via events or when modifying species with existing Artificial Psychic/Special Proprietor traits.
* [Tweak] Artificial psychic/special prosthetic traits can be added to the machine and synthetic populations.

## Issues still being fixed

There are a few issues I've noticed during play so far, some of which have been fixed, but others of which have not yet had time to be tweaked. The following is a to-do list of exploit fixes.

* Capitals traditionally added posts will not be added again after a capital move/loss.

If you find any other exploits/bugs/issues, feel free to give feedback in the comment section and I will fix them as soon as possible.

## Balance Adjustment

I think the balance adjustment belongs to the original author's right, I will not adjust the balance unless to fit the features removed/added by the new version, only the features that can improve the game experience will be improved, such as adjusting the continuation time of the decree, etc.

If you have any suggestions for the game experience, please feel free to give your feedback.

## Compatibility with other mods

The MoreTradition mod is great, but if you want to make it work with other great mods, fix conflicts, or adjust playability for content in other mods, feedback is also welcome. For example, for Real Space players, the ranges of all weapons have been adjusted for the new galaxy scale, while the miniature arc/light spear/brilliant energy emitter has not been adjusted.

Assuming you need to adapt the MoreTradition mods to other great mods, feel free to leave a comment and I'll be happy to provide compatibility support.

# 额外传统 - 重制版

曾经的[额外传统](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2616955720)是一个不可多得的优秀MOD，不仅在于多样的玩法，而且传统所增加的不仅仅是BUFF，而是更有代入感的游戏性，这才是最为玩家们看重的内容。

但是在3.4版本发布之后，[星劫](https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561198277755366)忙于考研，已经无力继续更新MOD，我曾经在评论区中、steam私信中试图与星劫大大取得联系，以便加入MOD维护与更新的工作中来，遗憾的是，没能得到原作者的回复。

因此，我会暂时发布一份适配最新版本stellaris的额外传统MOD，以便新版本的玩家们仍然能游玩到额外传统MOD，当星劫大大有时间继续完成自己的更新时，我会将此MOD删除。

## 相对于新版本的调整

**文物行动**

* [调整] 文物传统：全民鉴赏、文物传统：高调展览、文物传统：先驱经验的持续时间修改为时年，且会立即获得一定量凝聚力、影响力（如同原本文物行动决策）。

**舰船武器**

* [3.6] 辉煌能量发射器与其他XL槽武器一样，对战列舰造成双倍伤害，对泰坦造成三倍伤害。

**星球决策**

* [调整] 齐心传统：同心协力不再有持续时间限制，而是在启用时即生效，直到取消。移除同心协力将会花费100影响力，且对于星球上的每个人口都会失去1影响力。

**法令**

* [3.4] 法令现在消耗凝聚力而非影响力。
* [调整] 法令：特殊培养可以选择一次培养1个/5个/10个/25个领袖，并且前途无量的天才特质得到了补全，拥有所有可获得的特质（不包括大可汗、纳米个体、神谕等角色特有的特质）。
* [3.4] 政令传统：纵横寰宇和政令传统：雄伟壮丽的持续时间被修改为无限，但需要凝聚力进行维护。

**岗位**

* [修复] 修复了首都传统提供的精英岗位的优先级BUG，人口会自行上岗这些岗位，且比同类型的低级岗位优先级更高。

**传统**

* [调整] 所有的传统采用负面效果被更换为正面效果，花费凝聚力吃DEBUFF实难理解。
* [3.4] 狂想：修改了各项perk的功能，以使其适配新版本的巨构成本（凝聚力）。
* [调整] 陆军：小队协作perk的增加连带伤害改为减少连带伤害。
* [调整] 通灵：虚空产出perk改为百分比增加20%能量币产出，而非固定的20点。
* [3.4] 政令：培训制度perk改为减少帝国规模惩罚，智能行政perk改为减少人口与区划带来的帝国规模，灵活机构perk改为减少恒星系和殖民地带来的帝国规模，精简系统perk改为减少法令花费与维护费，广泛认可perk改为增加法令经费并减少法令花费与维护费。

**人口特质**

* [修复] 人工通灵/特殊义体特质不会直接显示在特质列表里，仅可通过事件添加，或修饰已有人工通灵/特殊义体特质的物种时添加。
* [调整] 人工通灵/特殊义体特质可以给机器人口与合成人口添加。

## 仍在修复中的问题

目前在游玩过程中我留意到了一些问题，其中一部分进行了修复，但其中另一部分尚未来得及进行调整。以下是漏洞修复的待办清单：

* 首都传统增加的岗位在迁都/失去首都后不会再次添加。

如果您发现了其他的漏洞/BUG/问题，欢迎在评论区进行反馈，我会尽快修复。

## 平衡性调整

我认为平衡性的调整是属于原作者的权利，除非为了适配新版本移除/增加的特性，否则我不会对平衡性进行调整，仅会对能够改善游戏体验的功能进行完善，例如调整法令的延续时间等。

如果您有对于游戏体验方面的建议，欢迎反馈。

## 与其他MOD的兼容性

额外传统MOD是一个优秀的MOD，如果您想让它与其他优秀的MOD进行协作，或是针对其他的MOD中的内容进行冲突性修复、可玩性调整，同样欢迎反馈。例如对于Real Space的玩家，所有武器的射程都应对新的星系比例进行了调整，而微缩电弧/光矛/辉煌能量发射器却未被调整。

假定您需要使更多传统MOD适配其他优秀的MOD，欢迎留言，我乐于提供兼容支持。
